Personal Profile and CV

Hello my name is Chris, and I'm a Masters Data Science Student, at The University of Essex Online. Skilled Data Analyst with experience using SQL, Stata, and Power BI. Current Data Analyst for 24x7ltd, who specialise in school transport for mainstream and special need users. The role has involved using specialist software QRoutes to create more efficient and effective routing. Using Microsoft Excel for analysing data. Also using a combination of SQL (MySQL) and PowerBI to create meaningful and insightful reports.

I have studied at the main campus of The University of Essex, attaining a first class honours degree in Economics. For my degree, I completed modules such as, Market structure and strategic behaviour, Economic analysis of asset prices, Experimental methods in economics, Public economics, Financial markets and intermediation, and Labour economics.
