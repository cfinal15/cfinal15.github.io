Personal Profile

Hello, my name is Chris, and I'm a Masters Data Science Student at The University of Essex Online. I’ve created this website through GitHub to showcase my postgraduate degree work. I have studied at the University of Essex beforehand, gaining a BSc in Economics.

Currently, I’m a Researcher for Essex County Council. I’m skilled in using qualitative and quantitative methods of research, to deliver an evidence-based approach in my work. My specialism is towards quantitative research/analysis and using visualisations to present findings. You can check out the work me and my team do, on Essex Open Data.

I have previous experience being a Data Analyst for the passenger transport company, 24x7 ltd. The role involved using specialist software QRoutes to create more efficient and effective routing. Along with using Microsoft Excel, SQL (MySQL) and PowerBI, to create meaningful and insightful reports.
